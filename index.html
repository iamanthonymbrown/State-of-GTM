Feature Comparison Notes

ICP Input Methods

Origami and Clay lead in flexibility — Origami lets you describe your ICP in natural language and the AI finds matching leads across the web. Clay uses a spreadsheet-based workflow where you define columns and enrichment steps. ZoomInfo offers AI-generated ICP from your CRM win/loss history. Sales Navigator uses LinkedIn’s professional graph with Persona matching and CSV upload. Apollo and Seamless.AI use traditional filter-based search. Persana offers AI agents + filters, while Bitscale combines templated playbooks with a spreadsheet interface.

Enrichment Depth

ZoomInfo has the largest proprietary database — 500M+ contacts and 100M companies with multi-source verification processing 1.5B+ data points daily. Clay leads in flexibility with 150+ data providers and waterfall enrichment. Sales Navigator is unique — it’s not a data provider but gives you access to LinkedIn’s 1B+ member graph with real-time professional data. Apollo relies on its own 275M+ contact database. Seamless.AI uses real-time AI web scanning.

Credit Systems

Most platforms use credit-based pricing. ZoomInfo is the outlier — annual contracts starting at ~$15K/yr with no monthly option. Sales Navigator is per-seat subscription with no credits. Apollo's credits expire monthly. Clay splits into Data Credits + Actions. Persana offers credit rollover. Seamless.AI charges per reveal even for inaccurate data. Watch for ZoomInfo’s credit overages — they can double your bill.

CRM Lock-In

ZoomInfo and Apollo offer the broadest CRM integration from their entry tiers — ZoomInfo includes Salesforce, HubSpot, and Dynamics on all paid plans. Apollo gives you Salesforce + HubSpot on $49/mo Basic. Sales Navigator adds CRM sync on Advanced ($160/mo) and full writeback on Advanced Plus. Clay moved CRM sync to $495/mo Growth. Bitscale locks HubSpot behind $349/mo and Salesforce behind $799/mo. Origami is CSV export only.
